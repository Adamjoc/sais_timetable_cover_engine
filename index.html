Loaded non-negotiables in this build

  • A2–A4 Spanish and ICT are blocked together; ICT runs as one double for alpha and one double for beta against Spanish.
  • A3 and A4 Maths and Science are intended to line up, with doubles preferred.
  • A2–A4 DT and Art run simultaneously by alpha / beta; PE is two doubles weekly.
  • A5–A6 iGCSE block with DT / Computer Science / ICT / Business Studies / Global Perspectives runs as one aligned block.
  • A5–A6 languages stay together; History and Geography stay together by year-group pairing; Science remains three doubles; English remains 2 singles + 1 double.
  • A-level details intentionally not guessed yet.

What this build is for

This is the “start to build” version: enough structure to let you begin shaping the timetable in-app, test the editing model, and prepare for the fully database-driven version once the remaining A-level and rooming constraints arrive.
